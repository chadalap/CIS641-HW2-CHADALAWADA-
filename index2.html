    Project Description

    Our project is all about building a Vehicle Inventory System that makes it easy for users to explore and compare cars from different brands. The idea came from our own experiences as international students struggling to understand the car market in the USA, where many brands and models were unfamiliar to us. We wanted to create a platform that helps others in similar situations. The system provides dedicated pages for each brand, showcasing models released between 2000 and 2024, and includes a tool for side-by-side comparisons. Users can also search and filter cars based on features like brand, year, or price, making it simple to find what they need. To keep the data secure, we added encryption to protect it from unauthorized access. Our goal is to give users especially newcomers like us the confidence to explore various options in cars.
